THE JANITORIAL FRONTIER

"In space, no one can hear you clean."

The year is 2847. You are Zyx-7, janitor aboard the luxury space station "Pristine Venture."

While everyone attended the Captain's Gala, you were cleaning the toilet in Cargo Bay 7. Again.

Then the alien artifact activated. Now the crew is unconscious, the AI has gone haywire, and the station is heading straight for the Blorgnax Homeworld.

Armed with your mop, your wits, and 15 years of janitorial expertise, you must save the day.

Type commands like LOOK, TAKE, USE, TALK TO, and GO NORTH to play.
